Nature

  Nature:

  The sun rises high in the sky,
  A fiery ball, as clouds drift by.
  The forest awakens, fresh and green,
  A vibrant tapestry, serene.
  
  The river flows, a winding stream,
  Reflecting trees, a mirrored dream.
  The mountains stand, a majestic sight,
  A testament to nature's gentle might.
  
  The wind whispers secrets, through the trees,
  A soothing melody, carried on the breeze.
  The earthy scent, of dampened ground,
  Invigorates senses, all around.
  
  In nature's embrace, I find my peace.
